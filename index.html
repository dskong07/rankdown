Rankdown
How good are you at estimating random bullshit?

How to Play

  1. 1
    Pick a category & set how many rounds you want (or go infinite).
  2. 2
    Guess items you think are in the ranked list and press Enter.
  3. 3
    Score points equal to the item's rank number. Rank #1 = 1 pt. Rank #73 = 73 pts.
  4. 4
    Wrong guesses use up a round. Hunt for obscure, high-ranked answers!
Strategy: Don't waste guesses on the obvious #1. Hunt for the obscure gold in the 40s–90s.
