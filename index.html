Rankdown
The Niche Knowledge Party Game

How to Play

  1. 1
    Pick a ranking category — countries by population, companies by market cap, and more.
  2. 2
    You have 20 guesses. Type any item you think is in the list and press Enter.
  3. 3
    Score points equal to the item's rank number. Rank #1 = 1 pt. Rank #47 = 47 pts.
  4. 4
    Wrong guesses still use up a round. Hunt for obscure, high-ranked answers!
💡 Strategy: Don't waste guesses on the obvious #1. Hunt for the obscure gold in the 30s and 40s.
